# Privacy Policy — Anki Translate Card

**Last updated:** March 2026

## What data does the extension access?

- **Selected text** on web pages (only when you right-click or press the keyboard shortcut)
- **Page URL and title** (to save the source link in your flashcard)

## Where does the data go?

- **Google Translate API** (`translate.googleapis.com`) — receives the selected word to generate translations. This is the same free API used by Google Chrome's built-in translation feature.
- **Your local Anki application** (`localhost:8765`) — receives the flashcard data via AnkiConnect. This stays on your computer.

## What the extension does NOT do

- Does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data
- Does not use cookies or tracking
- Does not send data to any third-party servers (other than Google Translate)
- Does not access your browsing history
- Does not run in the background — only activates when you explicitly create a flashcard

## Data storage

Your settings (selected languages and deck name) are saved locally in Chrome's extension storage (`chrome.storage.sync`). No data is stored externally.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/bocharsky/anki-translate-card/issues).
